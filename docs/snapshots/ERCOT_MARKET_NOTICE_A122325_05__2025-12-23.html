M-A122325-05 Large Load Batch Study Process Workshop #3 on February 26, 2026

NOTICE DATE: February 17, 2026

NOTICE TYPE: M-A122325-05 Operations

SHORT DESCRIPTION: Large Load Batch Study Process Workshop #3 on February 26, 2026

INTENDED AUDIENCE: Transmission Service Providers, Large Load Customers, and other interested stakeholders

DAYS AFFECTED: February 26, 2026

LONG DESCRIPTION: ERCOT will hold the next Large Load interconnection process workshop on February 26, 2026 to continue its work with stakeholders on a "Batch Study process" in connection with the Public Utility Commission of Texas (PUC) Project No. 59142, Review of ERCOT's Interconnection Processes for Large Loads.

As shared at the February 12, 2026 workshop, the PUC recently provided guidance to ERCOT and stakeholders to engage in an expedited process to develop revision requests that create a new Batch Zero study for consideration at the June 1, 2026 ERCOT Board meeting.  As such, ERCOT will be working with Technical Advisory Committee leadership to schedule workshops and special meetings as needed to meet this deadline.

ERCOT plans to share revision request concepts in support of Batch Zero at the February 26, 2026 workshop, which will be in-person and also available via WebEx and the Swagit broadcast.
