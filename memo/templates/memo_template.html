Scope & disclaimers

screening only
This memo is an audit-friendly screening artifact. It is not legal advice, and it does not replace ERCOT/TSP/TDSP engineering studies.
  • Not a schedule commitment: risk buckets are qualitative and rule-driven; they do not guarantee COD or energization timelines.
  • Not an interconnection determination: final requirements depend on studies, agreements, and stakeholder approvals (ERCOT / TSP / TDSP).
  • Inputs matter: missing or incorrect request inputs can materially change the evaluated path and checklist outcomes.
  • Evidence scope: citations reference public artifacts captured in the registry (doc_id + loc + sha256); interpretations may evolve as rules are updated.
